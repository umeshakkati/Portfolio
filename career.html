Rohit Sharma - Career Journey

Early Career (2005-2007)

Rohit Sharma started his cricketing journey in domestic cricket, impressing with a match-winning 142 in the Deodhar Trophy. His performances in the Ranji Trophy and U-19 World Cup set the foundation for his international career.

International Breakthrough (2007-2012)

  • ODI Debut: June 23, 2007, vs Ireland
  • T20I Debut: September 19, 2007, vs England
  • Played a key role in India’s **2007 ICC T20 World Cup** victory.

Becoming an Opener (2013-Present)

2013 was the turning point when Rohit was promoted as an opener. Since then, he has become one of the greatest ODI batsmen, scoring three double centuries and setting multiple world records.

IPL & Captaincy

  • Captain of **Mumbai Indians (MI)** since 2013.
  • Led MI to **5 IPL championships** (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020).
  • Appointed India’s **full-time captain** in 2022.
