Rohit Sharma - Achievements

Rohit Sharma has etched his name in cricket history with numerous records and achievements. From breaking batting records to leading India as captain, he continues to inspire millions.

🏆 Major Records & Achievements

  • 🔥 Only player to score 3 double centuries in ODIs
  • 🎯 Highest individual score in ODIs (264 vs Sri Lanka, 2014)
  • 🏏 Most centuries in a single World Cup (5 in 2019)
  • ⚡ Fastest T20I century (35 balls vs Sri Lanka, 2017)
  • 🏆 Led Mumbai Indians to 5 IPL Titles (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020)
  • ⭐ Captained India to victory in the 2023 Asia Cup

🎖 Awards & Recognition

  • 🏅 Arjuna Award (2015)
  • 🎖 Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award (2020)
  • 🏆 ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year (2019)
  • 🌟 Fastest Indian to 10,000 ODI runs as an opener
